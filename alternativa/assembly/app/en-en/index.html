About Us

OOO “Alternativa” is engaged in deliveries of ship and industrial equipment, and also spare parts for import, and national production from warehouses located in St. Petersburg, Moscow, and also abroad.

Our company appeared on the market in 2016.

At a present time we realize our deliveries to Saint Petersburg, Moscow, Murmansk, Novorossiysk, and also to the far East.

We coperate directly with factories in Russia, and also with foreign partners. We import goods to Russia by ourselves and issue all the necessary customs documents.

We offer a high quality goods at a reasonable price in the shortest delivery time. And we always try to be the most useful for our client. We strictly comply with our duties, provide all necessary documents and certificates for the delivered goods and bear all warranty obligations.
