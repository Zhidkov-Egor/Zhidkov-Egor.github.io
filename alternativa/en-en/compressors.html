Air and gas compressors and spare parts

An air compressor is a complex mechanism that requires periodic maintenance and repair. The largest number of breakdowns occurs in the most loaded nodes. For piston models, this is the piston block, engine, crankcase and cylinder, for screw models is the engine and screw block. The filter and connecting elements are subject to a lot of wear and tear, which also need periodic maintenance and replacement.

In OOO "Alternativa" assortment you will find everything you need to repair compressors. We offer a wide selection of air and oil filters, intake valves, gaskets, pressure sensors and other spare parts. These are original products of manufacturing plants-Renner, Ceccato, Abac, etc. It is characterized by high quality of manufacture and matching sizes.

Spare parts searching and purchasing is carried out by detailing, which will require clarification:

  • • compressor model;
  • • ser.Nr;
  • • manufacturing year.

If you do not have information about the detailing, our specialists will help with searching of required spares and repair kits for reciprocating, screw or diesel compressors.
