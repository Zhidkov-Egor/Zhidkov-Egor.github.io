Technical means of ventilation system elements

One of the activities of OOO “Alternativa” is supplying of ventilation equipment. We can offer a wide range of ventilation equipment to our customers that is necessary for air transportation in air supply and air conditioning systems, ventilation and ensuring consumption in boilers of marine vessels, as well as industrial and residential special facilities, whose requirements for the reliability of fans and service life are considered to be increased.

We supply all the necessary components for installation:

• Radial. In the RSS series, high, low, and medium pressure fans can be selected, depending on the requirements for specific application conditions. We also present the explosion-proof VRS series, which is effective for moving air containing gasoline, kerosene, and hydrogen vapors at temperatures up to +50 °C.

• Axial. The OS series offers easy-to-use and affordable fans that are resistant to corrosion, vibration and shock, hardy and able to work for a long time in difficult operating conditions. The explosion-proof VOS series guarantees high performance in transporting air masses with an admixture of hydrogen vapor and petroleum products at high temperatures.

We guarantee full-fledged consulting support, we will help to make any wholesale party at a favorable cost and promptly transport it to any city in Russia. Delivery of fans from the manufacturer is carried out to our company's own warehouse: the constant availability of the declared units of equipment is profitable for our customers and distinguishes us from other suppliers.
